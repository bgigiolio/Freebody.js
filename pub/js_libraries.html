Welcome to Freebody.js

Freebody.js is a project developed by Blake Gigiolio for CSC309.


The goal of Freebody.js is to empower physics students, teachers, and enjoyers in creating free body diagrams for websites. Freebody diagrams are critical and foundational to physics, occupying much of the curriculum for physics 1 in most universities, and ever present in k-12 physics education.
